Elyce Cole

Elyce Cole is a Master’s candidate (2019) in the Social-Organizational Psychology program at Teachers College, Columbia University. Previously, she graduated from George Washington University with a BBA, cum laude, in International Business and Marketing.

As an organizational development (OD) research practitioner, she specializes in group dynamics, specifically addressing covert group processes using a systems psychodynamic approach to analyze and enhance team performance.

Prior to pursuing her graduate studies, she worked as a sales and marketing strategist for a leadership training and OD consulting firm, to develop organizational culture change strategies for Fortune 500 companies in the healthcare, biopharmaceutical, IT, and finance industries.

Relevant to her studies, she is also a community mediator-in-training at the New York Peace Institute in Brooklyn, and an associate of The New York Center for the Study of Groups, Organizations and Social Systems, an affiliate of the A.K. Rice Institute.
